# Course 1: Foundations of Cybersecurity

## What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting organization's digital systems, networks, and sensitive information from attacks, unauthorized access, or damage.

The goal of cybersecurity is to protect sensitive information and ensure systems remain secure and operational.

## Why is Cybersecurity important?

As internet access and data volumes continue to rapidly expand globally, so do the digital threats facing people and organizations. This surge in risk has created a massive demand for cybersecurity professionals to protect vital systems and information.
